About Us

StudentNom is a web application that helps students find the best restaurants near their campus. Our mission is to provide students with a convenient way to discover new dining options, read reviews, and share their own experiences with others. Whether you're looking for a quick bite to eat or a place to celebrate a special occasion, StudentNom has you covered.
